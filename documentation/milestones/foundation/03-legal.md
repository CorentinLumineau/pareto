## Decision: Defer Until MVP Validation

**Rationale**: Focus on building and validating the product first.

Legal entity will be created when:
1. MVP is functional and deployed
2. First traffic/engagement proves concept
3. Ready to apply for affiliate programs
4. Ready to receive payments

## Tasks (When Ready)

### 1. SASU Registration

- [ ] Prepare documents (ID, address proof, statutes)
- [ ] Register online at guichet-entreprises.fr
- [ ] Pay registration fee (~200€)
- [ ] Wait for SIRET number (1-2 weeks)

### 2. Business Bank Account

- [ ] Choose online bank (Qonto, Shine, Revolut Business)
- [ ] Upload documents
- [ ] Deposit initial capital (1€ minimum for SASU)

### 3. JEI Status (Optional)

- [ ] Apply for Jeune Entreprise Innovante status
- [ ] Benefits: Tax reductions, social charge exemptions

## Budget (When Activated)

## Trigger Conditions
